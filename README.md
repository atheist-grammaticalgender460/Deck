## 📋 How to Use Deck

You start with a blank board. You create categories to group your tasks. You might name them To Do, In Progress, and Done. Add a card to a category by clicking the plus icon. You type your task details into the editor. Use the three-pane view to see your list on the left, your details in the middle, and your stats on the right.

### Managing Cards
You update a card by clicking on it. You change the title or the description at any time. When you finish a task, you drag the card into the Done section. The system records this change. You see your progress update in the stats pane.

### Viewing Stats
Click the charts icon to view your data. The application counts your active tasks. It shows how many items you finished this week. You see which categories hold the most work. These metrics help you decide what to do next.

## 🔒 Privacy and Data

Your privacy matters. Deck does not send your data to any server. No cloud storage exists for your boards. You manage your files locally. If you want to move your data to a new computer, you copy the folder where you saved your project. You own your information at all times.

## 💡 Tips for Efficiency

Keep your board clean. Remove old cards when you finish a project. Use descriptions to add notes to your tasks. You can add links or small text snippets to help you remember details. Change categories to match your workflow. You can name them based on effort, priority, or team members.
